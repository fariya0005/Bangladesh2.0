Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Idea

The foundation of progress begins with transformative ideas that inspire change and growth for a better Bangladesh.

Innovation

Fostering a culture of innovation to create technological advancements and sustainable solutions for future challenges.

Vision

A unified vision to transform Bangladesh into a hub of excellence, inclusivity, and global recognition.

Challenges

Overcoming barriers with resilience and determination to achieve a future marked by success and equity.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus emphasized creating a "new Bangladesh" built on democracy, transparency, and sustainable development. He highlighted the importance of reforms in governance, education, and economic systems to empower citizens and ensure justice. Yunus called for unity and global collaboration to achieve a brighter future for the nation.
